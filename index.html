About the Library

River Town Public Library offers books, ebooks, audiobooks, study spaces, and occasional cookie crumbs (please don’t tell Facilities). Everyone is welcome.

Featured Book

Title: The Map of Small Rivers

Author: A. Delaney

How to Get a Card

  1. Bring a photo ID and proof of address.
  2. Fill out the very small form.
  3. Pick a card design you’ll be proud to flash.
  4. Borrow something immediately.
